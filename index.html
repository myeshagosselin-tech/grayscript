Background

About GrayScript

A development practice built on two careers — fifteen years in direct social services, six years in entertainment. The combination is the point.

Before entering the entertainment industry, I spent fifteen years working directly in social services — with children, families, and communities navigating challenging life circumstances. That work taught me something no screenwriting course can: that people begin healing when they own their narratives. That tasting, touching, feeling, reconnecting to those narratives brings them home. A good story brings you home — even if you have never been there.

"Making feelings mentionable and manageable starts early — through connecting to your own voice and learning how to use it."

I transitioned into entertainment six years ago and have since served simultaneously as Story Editor, Producer, and Creative Executive on one of the highest-audience independent drama series ever produced — reaching more than 200 million viewers across 180 countries.

GrayScript exists because the best creative development is a conversation, not a transaction. I work with writers and projects directly, bringing an executive's eye and a human services foundation to every engagement.
